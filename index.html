Process & Materials

Considered, tactile and calm — every stage is finished by hand to preserve the softness of the stone.

●

Materials

Local limestone and hebel blocks, chosen for open grain and warmth.

▢

Process

Hand-carved with chisels and rifflers, refined through slow sanding.

⌖

Made to Order

Consultation, sketches, and measured lead times for bespoke works.
